1. SCOPE OF SERVICES

The Developer agrees to design, build, and deploy a corporate website for the Client, encompassing its three branches: C & T Tutors and Learning Centre, C & T Consultants, and C & T Web Design.

2. DURATION OF PROJECT

The project shall be completed within a period of five (5) weeks from the date of the first installment payment, provided all necessary content and assets are provided by the Client in a timely manner.

3. FINANCIAL TERMS

  • Total Contract Sum: MWK 450,000.00 (Four Hundred and Fifty Thousand Malawi Kwacha).
  • Initial Deposit: 50% (MWK 225,000.00) payable upon the signing of this Agreement.
  • Final Payment: 50% (MWK 225,000.00) payable ten (10) days after the official launch of the website.
  • Excluded Costs: The Client shall be responsible for all third-party costs, including but not limited to:
    • Domain registration
    • Web hosting
    • API integrations
    • Professional photography

4. RESPONSIBILITIES OF THE PARTIES

  • The Developer: Shall ensure the website is professional, mobile-responsive, and reflects the agreed-upon demo aesthetic.
  • The Client: Shall provide the Developer with all necessary brand assets (logos, text content, staff profiles) and shall be responsible for the final approval of the site before deployment.

5. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

Upon receipt of the final payment, the full ownership and intellectual property rights of the website's front-end design and content shall be transferred to the Client. The Developer retains the right to display the project in their professional portfolio.

6. CONFIDENTIALITY

Both parties agree to keep all business information, financial data, and trade secrets disclosed during this project strictly confidential.

7. TERMINATION

Either party may terminate this agreement with seven (7) days' written notice.

  • If the Client terminates, the Developer shall be entitled to payment for work completed up to the date of termination.
  • The initial deposit remains non-refundable if work has already commenced.

8. GOVERNING LAW

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the Laws of the Republic of Malawi. Any disputes arising shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the Courts of Malawi.
