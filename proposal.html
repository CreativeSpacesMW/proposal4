1. Executive Summary

C & T Group of Partners is a multifaceted firm providing essential financial, educational, and technical services in Malawi. To reflect your status as a Certified QuickBooks Online ProAdvisor and a leader in professional tutoring (ACCA, ICAM), a premium, high-performance digital headquarters is required.

Creative Spaces MW proposes the development of a professional corporate website that unifies your three branches—Tutors, Consultants, and Web Design—into a seamless, credible user experience.

2. The Objective

Currently, there is a gap between the high-level expertise C & T offers and its online visibility. We will bridge this gap by:

  • Consolidating Brand Identity: Creating a unified home for your three distinct branches.
  • Building Authority: Showcasing your certifications and professional tutoring success.
  • Lead Generation: Making it easy for students and corporate clients to book consultations or enroll in courses.

3. Project Scope & Deliverables

I have developed three custom demos specifically tailored to your aesthetic. The final project includes:

Core Deliverables

  • Custom Web Design: A mobile-responsive, professional layout that adapts seamlessly across all devices.
  • Service Integration: Dedicated sections for:
    • Consultancy Services (Tax/Audit/QuickBooks)
    • Tutors & Learning Centre (ACCA/MBA/ICAM)
    • IT & Web Design Services
  • Deployment: Full setup and launch on your preferred hosting environment.
  • QuickBooks/Sage Highlight: Specialized UI elements to showcase your software expertise and certification.
